Student prototype for Maggie

A cleaner way to keep up with swim lessons.

I mocked this up around a separate private-lessons calendar that sits on my own account. The point was not to make a huge polished platform. I wanted something an instructor could use fast after a lesson, while giving Maggie one place to check what happened this week.
